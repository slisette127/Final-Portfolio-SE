My name is Sandra.
I am a wife and step-mother to an amazing 10 year old. I believe that it is never too late to start over. To borrow a quote from Sir Ulrich von Liechtenstein, "A man can change his stars." With kindness and a whole lot of determination, anything is possible. Being adaptable and inquisitive keeps my life from becoming tedious. There is always something to learn or experience. Failure has never been a deterrent. Everyone fails at some point in their lives. It is just as much apart of the journey as succeeding, albeit not as enjoyable.

"You must prepare for life, and whatever it will throw at you." - Moira Rose

I started my creative journey at a young age by wanting to become a fashion designer. That desire led me to Nashville, TN where I attained a bachelor's degree in Fashion Design and Merchandising. After a few years my desire to spread my wings led me to Los Angeles, CA. Having aquired the technical skills from the fashion world, it was an easy leap to become a professional seamstress for tv and film. My work has traveled the world and you would never know it was made by me. Being behind the scenes has always been where I prefer to be. I enjoy helping others shine. After almost a decade in the sun, it was time to return to Texas. Here I ventured into the world of Interior Design.For the past three years I have worked as a Regional Decorator for a chain of furniture stores. This year, I decided it was time for a complete change. I wanted to have a career that would challenge me as well as withstand pandemics and recessions.
